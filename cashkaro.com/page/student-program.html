Frequently Asked Questions

What is CashKaro?

CashKaro.com is India's largest Cashback & Coupons Website. It helps you save on all your regular purchases across 1500+ shopping websites. Earning Cashback is simple, just type the name of the retailer you want to shop at and avail interesting discounts along with additional cashback.

What will I do as a CashKaro Student Representative?

As a CashKaro Student Representative, your main roles are:

  • Represent CashKaro at your campus and understand your peers shopping needs.
  • Promote amazing deals & discounts with the student community.
  • Implement new ideas/strategies to get maximum number of transactions.
  • Scale various student programs and campaigns

Who can apply?

Applications for Student Representative Program (SRP) are open to all college-going students of all degrees, streams and years. Any college student who is a marketing enthusiast, and who wants to experience the excitement of working with No. 1 Cashback & Coupon Website in India, can apply for this program.

What is the duration of SRP?

SRP is a 90-day work from home program starting from August till October.

Do I have to pay anything for applying to SRP or after getting selected?

No, you do not have to pay anything either for applying to the SRP or after getting selected.

How much cash incentive can I earn as a Student Representative?

Student Representative Program is a performance based incentive program. There is no limit to what you can earn in this program.

Will I get a certificate which I can add on my resume?

Yes, Ofcourse! On successful completion of the internship, you will be rewarded with the completion letter/certificate or even a prestigious letter of recommendation from our founder which you can add on your resume to flex a little more.

How many hours do I need to devote as a Student Representative?

We would ideally want you to work 4 to 5 hours every week. But then again, working hours are completely dependent upon your availability.

I have filled out the application form, what are the next steps?

Congratulations on successfully applying to the Student Representative Program. We will get back to you over email for further process.

Do I need to travel anywhere for the Student Representative Program?

No, there is no need for you to travel anywhere. The CRP is a work from Home program. All the tasks and activities for the CRP are required to be performed From your Home or College Campus.

I have more questions about the program that have not been answered here – what do I do?

Email us your queries at kartikay@cashkaro.com. We would be happy to assist you. Please note that the average response time for all SRP hiring-related queries is between 48 to 72 hours.
