How Do We Choose the Best Mobiles for You?

Mobiles are a necessity in today’s age, so it is important to know about the best mobile brand before buying one. Good mid-range mobile phones come in the range of Rs 7,000 to Rs 15,000. We have also taken care of user reviews and their experience in selecting the best Indian mobile companies.

When selecting the best mobile phone for your needs, we consider a variety of crucial factors to ensure that you get a device that not only meets but exceeds your expectations. Here’s how we do it.

Assessing User Needs

We start by categorising phones based on user profiles. Indian mobile companies offer amazing mid-range phones that are perfect for users who prioritise communication and basic apps.

Battery Performance

Knowing the frustration of a quickly draining battery, we prioritise models with long-lasting battery life, especially for power users. We look for a minimum of 1000mAh, going higher for phones designed for heavy usage.

Memory and Storage Options

RAM: We selected phones offering a minimum of 4GB RAM for efficient multitasking and smooth performance, with higher RAM options for gaming and intensive tasks.

Storage: We recommend a minimum of 64GB internal storage, considering the needs for app data, photos, and videos, and favour phones with expandable storage options.

Display

Additionally, we favoured phones with vibrant displays so that you can experience good colour accuracy and viewing angles.

We considered keypad phones to have quality LCD screens, which offer a good viewing experience. Also, while considering touch screens, minimum Full HD resolution is our standard.

Customer Feedback

We also heavily weigh customer reviews as well as expert opinions to gauge satisfaction levels and any common issues or standout features.

FAQs

Which is the no.1 mobile brand in India?

Xiaomi stands as the top-selling phone brand in India with over 40 million users. If you’re looking for a brand that offers great quality within a decent budget range, you should definitely check out Xiaomi.

Which phone is the most used in the world?

Currently, iPhone by Apple tends to be among the most used phones globally. They have consistently been popular choices worldwide and are known for their premium designs.

Which phone has the best camera quality?

Today, there are numerous devices that feature great camera quality but some still stand out from the lot. If you’re looking for a phone which has a premium camera, you can go for the iPhone series and above or any latest OnePlus devices to get your hands on flagship mobile cameras.

Which phone is the most expensive?

The Falcon Supernova iPhone 6 Pink Diamond phone in the world is one of them. With a $48.5 million price tag, it is presently the priciest phone ever.

Is Apple better than Samsung?

Both brands have some of the best quality smartphones on the market. Apple offers you the option to sync and manage data on all of your Apple devices. Whereas, Samsung works exceptionally well with third-party applications. The camera quality in both devices is supreme. However, you may get more depth and intensity in high-end Samsung phones.

Who introduced mobile phones in India?

The first mobile phone call in India was made between West Bengal Chief Minister Jyoti Basu and then-Union Telecom Minister Sukh Ram. The call was made on a Nokia mobile phone and the airwaves were by Modi-Telstra. The first call was made on July 31, 1995.

Which mobile phones are made in India?

A lot of India’s top smartphone manufacturers such as Xiaomi, Samsung, and Realme make their smartphones in India. Apart from assembling smartphones, they also manufacture certain internal parts at factories in India.

Which phone is popular in India?

In India, popular phone brands include Samsung, Xiaomi, OnePlus, and Apple, among others. The latest flagship phones from these brands are generally the most popular choices.
