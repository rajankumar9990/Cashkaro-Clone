How to Choose the Right Size in Men's Innerwear?

Here are some tips to help you choose the right size in men's innerwear, ensuring a perfect blend of comfort and support.

  • Understand the Sizing Chart: Brands like XYXX Crew offer sizing charts on their website listing sizes based on waist measurements. Before buying, accurately measure your waist with a tape measure to find the right size.
  • Consider the Fabric: The fabric of innerwear, such as Lenzing MicroModal used by XYXX Crew, impacts fit due to its stretchiness and softness. While it offers more flexibility, it's crucial to follow the size chart for optimal comfort.
  • Know Your Preferences: Preference in fit varies individually. Some men opt for a snug fit for support, particularly during athletic pursuits, while others lean towards a relaxed fit for daily comfort. Tailor your choice based on your activities and personal comfort.
  • Check for Special Features: Consider innerwear with features like a contoured pouch for added support and a no-roll waistband for better fit and comfort when selecting your size.
  • Read Customer Reviews: Customer reviews are valuable for sizing insights. Look for comments on whether the innerwear runs large, small, or true to size to make a more informed decision.
  • Try Different Styles: Various types of innerwear fit differently on the body, such as briefs, trunks, and boxer briefs. Experiment with different styles in your size to discover the most comfortable option for you. XYXX Crew provides a range of choices to help you find the perfect fit and style.
  • When in Doubt, Size Up: When in doubt or between sizes, it's best to opt for a larger size for added comfort, especially if you prefer looser fitting innerwear. Aim for a balance between support and comfort.
