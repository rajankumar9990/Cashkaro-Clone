Cancellations, Return, & Refund Policy

With JioMart, cancellations, returns, and refunds are easy. You can cancel your order prior to its dispatch by the seller or Reliance. However, once the dispatch has been initiated, the order cannot be cancelled. The request for cancellation can then be made by cancelling the order from your ‘My Orders’ section. Once your cancellation request is received, the refund process will be initiated within 24 hours of confirmation. JioMart’s return timeline and conditions vary for different products and categories, so it is essential to check their policy for eligible returns.
