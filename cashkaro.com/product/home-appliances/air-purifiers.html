How To Choose a Good Air Purifier?

Filters

Air purifiers come with different types of filters - HEPA, Carbon, and Pre-filters. The HEPA filters remove dust particles and allergens like mould. A Carbon filter purifier removes fumes, filters smoke, and filters out other chemicals. Both improve the air quality.

Usage

Air purifiers have massive benefits. They are designed for allergy relief as the HEPA filters can remove indoor allergens. If you or your loved ones suffer from asthma, go for the asthma-specific air purifiers. Smoke air purifiers are good for places with a fireplace, smoke releasing industries nearby, and more.

Capacity/Size

The size of the room is to be considered. A larger room will require an air purifier that can run for extended periods of time. Go for a purifier that can work in a room which is estimated to be larger than your current room. The packaging mentions the area which it is suitable for.
