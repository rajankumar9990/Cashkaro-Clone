List Of Top Beauty Care Brands In India

Nivea

Nivea has established itself as one of the best beauty labels in the world, with an experience in skincare that goes over 130 years. At Nivea, each product is designed to deliver premium skincare to all types of skins such as oily, dry, or sensitive, at affordable prices. Nivea was one of the first brands to identify skin issues that evolve with time and hence created a wholesome array of body care products for intensive nourishment.

Lakme

Lakme is the oldest Indian cosmetics brand that produces quality beauty products at prices that can be bought by most. No wonder, it is one of the most popular beauty names in any household across the country. An ever-evolving brand, Lakme beauty products for faces like foundations, BB and CC creams, concealers etc., suit most Indian skin tones. Some of the most loved Lakme beauty products are the Lakme Absolute 9 to 5 cream, Lakme Perfect Radiance Cream, and the Lakme Absolute Youth Infinity Cream, amongst many others.

Colorbar

Colorbar is one of India leading beauty brands, a brand that celebrates femininity through its range of products. Launched in 2004, Colorbar offers a premium range of beauty products for skincare and makeup, whilst catering to the diverse requirements of different individuals. Over the years, Colorbar has crafted an ardent niche in supplying the latest beauty products that gel with the needs of Indian women, and are suitable for varying climates and lifestyles. Each of Colorbars products has been designed keeping in mind the intricacies and problems of the Indian skin, making them a go-to brand for lakhs of women.

Forest Essentials

Forest Essentials is a premier Ayurvedic skincare brand that primarily blends traditional and modern beauty techniques to deliver products that are excellent for the skin. Founded in India in the year 2000, Forest Essentials bathing products are exquisite, organic, and cruelty-free, made from only natural ingredients. Some of their most popular products are their face ubtans, essential oils, face creams, and hair cleansers that are enriched with premium ingredients. Catering to the beauty needs of men and women, VLCC produces some of the finest beauty products in India.
