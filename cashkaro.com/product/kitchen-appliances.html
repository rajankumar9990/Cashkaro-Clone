List of Best Kitchen Appliance Brands in India

Godrej

Established in 1897, Godrej is a reputed brand that is loved by 1.1 billion consumers globally. Although it is into several business verticals, it is one of the best brands for kitchen appliances. In fact, in 1958, it was the first brand to make an Indian refrigerator with which it began its journey into the kitchen appliances segment.

Prestige

Another leading name in the domain of home appliances, Prestige takes pride in manufacturing some of the best kitchen appliances in India. Their Prestige Xclusive range of products have been designed to make kitchens smarter, cleaner, and more hygienic. Prestige has appliances such as cookers, electric cooktops, mixer grinders, juicers, and sandwich makers.

LG

LG is known for striking the perfect balance between the inclusion of high-tech features and dependability. From washers and dryers to other home appliances such as vacuum cleaners, microwaves, and refrigerators, LG offers an array of supremely efficient home appliances that feature a host of advanced resource-saving technology.

IFB

IFB is seen as a world-class home appliances brand that caters to a wide section of the Indian population. With a range of diverse products, IFB kitchen appliances sport easy-to-use features. IFB has a range of seamless kitchen appliances such as microwaves, mixer grinders, juicers, and ovens. Many experts also consider IFBs technology to be superior to that of its foreign counterparts in the low-range and mid-range categories.

Morphy Richards

Morphy Richards is counted among the top 5 most popular kitchen appliance brands in India and is a sub-extension of the Bajaj group. It produces electronic appliances that are ideal for the Indian kitchen. Their diverse range of kitchen appliances include juicers, sandwich makers, electric cooktops, hot water geysers, coffee makers, and hand blenders.
